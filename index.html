Mission Statement

To further popularize illustrated naratives, and provide exposure to stories that are worth being seen. As demonstrated with an existing work below, excessively professional and detailed draftsmanship is not required to find great success. There are other factors such as the themes backing a work that make it valuable.

User Stories

Cultivation

As a creator/contributer I want to sign up for an account that allows me to upload my works to a public page that organizes works in different categories(such as Up Votes, Views etc.), where works that excel in a category are listed first in that category. Through uploading content that excels in certain categories I will find an audience.

Libraries

As a consumer/visitor, I want to select and view content from a catalogue of illustrated works organized into categories. From this I have free access to a large library of original comics with easy access to the most popular, discussion provoking works.

Discussion

As a consumer/critic, I want to visit links to discussion threads relevant to the content I view. From this I will engage in discussion for any work that inspires words from me. I seek to discuss about a variety of topics relating to the work including themes and criticism.
